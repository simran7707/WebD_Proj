About Us

The food door showcases inventive Indian cuisine by complementing the flavours and traditions of India with global ingredients and techniques. The Food Door , New Delhi is ranked as the #22 Restaurant in Asia by Asias 50 Best Restaurants. It has been awarded the San Pellegrino Best Restaurant in India by Asias 50 Best Restaurants for seven consecutive years (from 2015-2021).
